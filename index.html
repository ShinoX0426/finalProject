Drawing

Drawing in art includes characteristics such as line, shape, form, value, perspective, and composition. An art drawing may include materials such as paper, ink, graphite, and charcoal.

art 2

Painting

the expression of ideas and emotions, with the creation of certain aesthetic qualities, in a two-dimensional visual language. The elements of this language—its shapes, lines, colours, tones, and textures—are used in various ways to produce sensations of volume, space, movement, and light on a flat surface.

art 3

Handicrafts

sometimes more precisely expressed as artisanal handicraft or handmade, is any of a wide variety of types of work where useful and decorative objects are made completely by one's hand or by using only simple, non-automated related tools like scissors, carving implements, or hooks.

art 4

Collage

an art form that is made up of overlapping pieces of material, such as photographs, fabric, coloured and textured paper and other types of mixed media.

Acrylic Paints

Acrylics can be applied in thin layers or washes to create effects that resemble watercolors and other water-based mediums.

Cartoon Drawings

Cartoon is a type of visual art that is typically drawn, frequently animated, in an unrealistic or semi-realistic style.

Caricature Drawings

Drawing of a person or thing in which the features and form have been distorted and exaggerated in order to mock or satirise the subject.
